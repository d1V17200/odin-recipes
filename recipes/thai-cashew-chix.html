Thai Cashew Chicken

Description

This cashew chicken makes for a quick weekday meal. It is super easy to put together and tastes as good as your local takeout place. This is favorite staple of mine.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1lb boneless skinless chicken tenders or chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2tsp paprika
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1/2tsp cumin
  • 2TB oilive oil
  • 1/4 cup lite soy sauce
  • 2 garlic gloves, diced

Cashew Sauce

  • 1/2 cup unsalted roasted cashews
  • 1/2 cup lite soy sauce<
  • 2TB hoisin
  • 1TB oyster sauce
  • 1/2TB gochujang
  • 1TB ginger paste
  • 1TB brown sugar
  • 2TB water
  • Drizzle of honey, to taste
  • 2tsp all purpose flour

Vegetables

  • 2 bell peppers, any kind. Cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 jumbo white onion cut into bite size pieces
  • 2TB olive oil

How To make

  1. De-tendon your chicken, pat with paper towels to dry, and cut into bite-size chunks
  2. Combine dry ingredients in bowl or shallow dish
  3. toss chicken in dry ingredients until well-coated
  4. Heat a 12in pan over medium-high heat, add olive oil
  5. Once olive oil is shimmering, add chicken pieces
  6. Cook chicken on med-high for 4-5 minutes until browned and internal temp registers 165F. Flipping half-way thru.
  7. Once cooked thru, about 4-6 minutes, set heat to low and add garlic, cook 1min, then de-glaze pan with 1/4 cup soy sauce and remove from heat.
  8. In the meantime, combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk together
  9. Heat large pan over med-high heat. Add olive oil
  10. Once oil is shimmering, add vegetables and saute until lightly browned. About 5 minutes
  11. Add cashews and saute until starting to brown, about 2 minutes
  12. Once vegetables are cooked, add sauce ingredients
  13. Reduce heat to med-low and let sauce simmer until thickened, about 4 minutes.
  14. Add chicken pieces and stir to combine
  15. Serve over jasmine or basmati rice, make sure to scoop extra sauce
