Roasted Red Pepper Pistachio Pesto

Description

I have been making this pesto for years. After getting bored of plain basil basil pesto I wanted to change it up. This pairs well with raviolis or dipping with your favorite cracker.

Ingredients

  • .4oz fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese. Alternatively, 2TB nutritional yeast for dairy free
  • 1 cup shelled pistachios
  • 2x12oz jars of roasted red peppers drained, rinsed, and patted dry
  • At least 2 cloves of garlic (as much as you want, really)
  • 1/2tsp cumin powder
  • 1tsp chili powder
  • 1/2tsp onion powder
  • 2TB lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

How To make

  1. Place all ingredients except for the EVOO in a food processor or high-power blender
  2. Pulse in 1sec increments until smooth and creamy
  3. Once well incorporated, turn processor to blend and drizzle in EVOO for 10 seconds, about 1/4 cup
  4. Season with salt and pepper, to taste
