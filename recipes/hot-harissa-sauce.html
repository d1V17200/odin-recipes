Hot Harissa Sauce

This is a recent creation of mine. It is spicy and creamy and pairs well with anything mediterranean.

Description

This is a quick recipe for a spicy harissa-based sauce that pairs great with mediterranean meals or just used for dipping

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup hot Harissa paste
  • 2TB tahini
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1tsp chili powder
  • 1tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp onion powder

How To make

  1. Place all ingredients into a bowl or sauce dish
  2. Using a fork, mix until well incorporated
